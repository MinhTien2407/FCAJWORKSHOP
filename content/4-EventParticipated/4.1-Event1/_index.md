# Summary Report of AI & Cloud Seminar Series

## Purpose of the Seminar Series

- Explore the latest trends in Artificial Intelligence, Generative AI, and Cloud Computing.
- Learn from AWS experts and industry professionals through real-world case studies.
- Introduce modern AI-powered development tools and cloud-native architectures.
- Enhance knowledge of system design, software development, and enterprise AI solutions on AWS.

---

## Speakers

- **Anh Tịnh** – *Build Second Brain*
- **Hải Anh** – *Friendly AI Assistant with Amazon Q*
- **Thịnh** – *From Edge to Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation*
- **Team VIB** – *36 Hours with LotusHacks – Building UTM*
- **Đào Đức** – *Deep Dive Talk: How LLM Actually Works?*
- **Cát Vy** – *Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent System*

---

## Key Highlights

### Build Second Brain

- Learned how to organize personal knowledge efficiently.
- Applied the Second Brain methodology to improve productivity.
- Explored effective note-taking and knowledge management techniques.

### Friendly AI Assistant with Amazon Q

- Introduced the capabilities of Amazon Q for software development.
- Demonstrated how AI can assist with coding, documentation, and troubleshooting.
- Showed practical ways to improve developer productivity using AI.

### From Edge to Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation

- Learned the architecture and benefits of Amazon CloudFront.
- Understood how Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) improve application performance.
- Explored security features and global content delivery strategies.

### 36 Hours with LotusHacks

- Shared the experience of participating in an AI Hackathon.
- Demonstrated rapid product development under tight deadlines.
- Highlighted teamwork, project management, and MVP development practices.

### Deep Dive Talk: How LLM Actually Works?

- Explained how Large Language Models generate responses.
- Learned about tokens, probability prediction, temperature, and non-determinism.
- Discussed best practices when developing applications powered by LLMs.

### Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent System

- Introduced enterprise Multi-Agent AI architecture.
- Demonstrated collaboration among multiple AI agents to solve complex tasks.
- Presented practical enterprise use cases for Agentic AI systems.
