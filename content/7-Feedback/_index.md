> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation


### 1. Learning and Working Environment

The First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) program provided me with a professional, collaborative, and highly motivating learning environment. Throughout the internship, participants were always willing to support one another when facing challenges during workshops or hands-on labs. The structured learning roadmap made it easy for me to follow the training schedule and complete each learning objective effectively.

### 2. Support from Mentors and Program Organizers

The mentors were enthusiastic, knowledgeable, and always willing to answer questions whenever I encountered difficulties. They not only explained technical concepts clearly but also shared valuable real-world experiences working with AWS. The program organizers consistently provided learning materials, workshop updates, and timely announcements, allowing me to focus on learning without unnecessary obstacles.

### 3. Relevance to My Major

The training content closely matched my Information Technology background while introducing me to many modern cloud technologies. During the internship, I was able to apply my knowledge of computer networks, operating systems, databases, and programming to real AWS environments. In addition, I gained practical experience with DevOps, Serverless Computing, Containers, and Cloud Security.

### 4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities

The workshops and hands-on labs significantly improved both my technical and professional skills. I gained practical experience in deploying cloud infrastructure, managing AWS services, automating infrastructure with Infrastructure as Code (IaC), building CI/CD pipelines, designing Serverless applications, and implementing security best practices. I also enhanced my problem-solving, teamwork, technical writing, and time management skills.

### 5. Community Culture and Team Spirit

FCAJ fosters a positive learning community where participants actively share knowledge and support one another. Regular workshops and technical discussions encouraged collaboration and continuous learning. This supportive atmosphere motivated me to improve my technical skills throughout the internship.

### 6. Training Program

The training roadmap was well organized, starting with AWS fundamentals before progressing to more advanced topics such as Security, Containers, DevOps, Data Analytics, and Cost Optimization. Every topic included practical labs, allowing me to reinforce theoretical knowledge through real-world implementation.

## Additional Questions

### What was the most satisfying part of your internship?

The most rewarding aspect of my internship was gaining hands-on experience with the AWS ecosystem. Instead of only learning theoretical concepts, I had the opportunity to deploy, configure, monitor, and manage real cloud services, giving me a much deeper understanding of cloud computing in practical environments.

### What improvements would you suggest for future interns?

I would recommend adding more team-based projects or capstone projects that integrate multiple AWS services into a complete solution. Additionally, inviting experienced Cloud Engineers or Solutions Architects to share industry insights would provide interns with a better understanding of real-world cloud engineering practices.

### Would you recommend this program to your friends? Why?

Yes. I would highly recommend the First Cloud AI Journey program because it offers a clear learning roadmap, high-quality hands-on workshops, experienced mentors, and practical AWS training. The program provides an excellent foundation for anyone interested in pursuing a career in Cloud Computing, DevOps, or Cloud Engineering.

## Suggestions and Future Expectations

* I hope the program will continue expanding its advanced workshops, especially in Kubernetes, AI/ML, cloud architecture, and enterprise-level AWS services.

* More collaborative team projects would help participants strengthen their teamwork and software development skills in real-world scenarios.

* Additional career-sharing sessions from experienced Cloud Engineers, DevOps Engineers, and Solutions Architects would be valuable for career development.

* I hope FCAJ continues to update its learning materials to keep pace with the latest AWS technologies and industry best practices.

* I would be delighted to participate in future FCAJ programs and community activities to further improve my cloud computing knowledge and gain more practical experience.
